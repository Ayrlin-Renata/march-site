Immediate Preview & Labeling

Real-time folder watching means your newest captures appear instantly. Automatically group images taken in quick succession with Burst Grouping, and navigate your library with ease.

  • visibility Multi-folder watching & Burst grouping
  • label Quick cycle labeling with right-click
  • zoom_in High-performance lightbox with pan/zoom

02

Social Media Image Post Planning

Prepare your stories and image posts for X, Bluesky, and more. Use the Story Builder to drag images into layout slots and manage captions with the Text Preset Manager.

  • grid_view Platform-specific layouts (X, Bluesky, etc.)
  • content_paste Template & Preset manager for hashtags/emojis
  • aspect_ratio Quick crop and slot assignment (1-9 hotkeys)
Social Media Planning Image
03

Hold, Drag & Drop Sharing

No more digging through folders. Hold left-click on any thumbnail to immediately drag it into Discord, your browser, or any external program.

  • drag_pan Seamless external sharing
  • groups Instant Discord uploads
  • flash_on Zero-latency workflow
